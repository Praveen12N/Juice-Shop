Our Story

Founded in 2015, FreshSqueeze began with a simple mission: to provide our community with the freshest, most nutritious juices possible. We source organic produce from local farms and cold-press our juices to preserve maximum nutrients.

Our team of wellness experts carefully crafts each recipe to deliver both incredible flavor and health benefits. No additives, no preservatives - just pure, natural goodness.
